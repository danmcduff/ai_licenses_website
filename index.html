Responsible AI

Responsible AI

The AI Licensing intitiative aims to empower developers to restrict the use of their AI technology by developers and end-users in applications or methods that the creator considers irresponsible use of their technology.

Licensing Team FAQs

Licenses

We have created easy to use and legally enforceable AI Licenses that can be distributed with code, datasets or services (including APIs, SDKs). These licenses allow the use of the artifacts to be restricted in certain domains. In the absence of laws specifically dealing with AI we leverage existing copyright law for enforceability.


End User License Agreements

EULAs are aimed for distribution with software tools and datasets.

Developer Licenses

DLs are aimed for distribution with code and algorithms.
